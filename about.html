I'm Rana Elgohary; an introverted Egyptian / British designer. I was reincarnated from the romanticism era, probably part of me is still there. I design websites and user interfaces. I also have experience with photography, filmmaking, motion graphics and print design.

February 2014, I attended the Awwwards 'Love Days' Conference in Paris. I met a lot of very interesting people.

Summer 2012, I worked in Berlin with these lovely people. Spending the summer in Berlin was inspirational and eye-opening for me. I learned a lot from the internship and I definitely enjoyed the solitude, the food, the greenery and above all the long bicycle rides.

When I'm not working I'm usually spending time with my little brother and my tiny super fluffy dog. They're the best, they really are.

I developed a passion for art when I was 12. Back then I used to draw and decided I'm going to be an artist, eventually I went to design school and graduated in spring 2014.

I can provide work in concept development, wireframes, branding, application design, web design and front-end development: HTML and CSS (responsive).

If you have a freelance project for me, a job offer or simply want to introduce yourself then don't hesitate to send me a message and I'll delightedly and eagerly get back to you.

Captify at Dmexco

Captify is a British-founded ad tech company leading search retargeting in Europe. DIG has worked on the branding for the initial launch and has been working with Captify since. This year they took part in the Dmexco exhibition and DIG joined in on the fun! We designed the booth, the furniture, digital content, prints and even the merchandise.



Designed at DIG.
In collaboration with Mads Holst, Charlotte Ledger, Chloé Husson
and Adam Connolly.
